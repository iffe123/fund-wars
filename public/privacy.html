Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Fund Wars is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data needed to run the game. We don't sell your data. You can delete everything anytime.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information (Optional)

  • If you sign in with Google: your email address and display name
  • Guest mode requires no personal information

Game Data

  • Your game progress, statistics, and achievements
  • In-game choices and conversation history with AI characters

Analytics (Anonymous)

  • General usage patterns and session length
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Device type and operating system (no device identifiers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game functionality: Save your progress across devices
  • AI conversations: Power the NPC dialogue system via Google Gemini API
  • Improvements: Understand how players use the game to make it better

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and cloud save storage
  • Google Gemini AI: Powers NPC conversations
  • Vercel: Website hosting and analytics

4. Your Rights

  • Play without an account: Use guest mode with no data collection
  • Delete your data: Use "Reset Game" in settings, or contact us
  • Request your data: Email us and we'll provide what we have

5. Children's Privacy

Fund Wars is rated 12+ and contains mature business themes. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app.
